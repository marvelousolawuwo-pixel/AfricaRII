Who We Are

Empowering rural communities since 2012 through education, mentorship, and sustainable initiatives

Our Journey

Africa-RII started in 2012 as Train A Child Today (TACT), providing school supplies to underserved communities in Nigeria. Over the years, we have expanded our programs to include mentorship, scholarships, skill development, and community resource centers.

With a deep belief in restoring human dignity through education, skills, and mentorship programs as an empowerment strategy, Africa-RII has grown from providing school supplies to establishing a Community Resource Center in Ilora‚Äîa safe space for learning, skill acquisition, and mentorship.

In 2019, we launched free annual summer schools, JAMB and university tuition scholarships, and youth empowerment initiatives addressing literacy, higher education access, gender equality, and economic opportunities for young adults.

Today, Africa-RII is committed to driving long-term change through education, skill acquisition, healthcare awareness, and economic empowerment programs. By working at the intersection of education, health, economic sustainability, and youth development, we continue to create lasting change, empowering individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and build a future of opportunity and resilience.

Our Values

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to building a sustainable legacy of intergenerational impact through education and empowerment. Every program we run is designed to create lasting change that benefits not just today's youth, but future generations.

Our Strength

Community-led solutions, research, and collaboration drive our grassroots social change efforts. We believe in empowering local voices and working alongside communities to identify and address their unique challenges.

Our Team

Interdisciplinary experts united by transparency, reflection, and innovative approaches to social impact. Our diverse team brings together educators, healthcare professionals, community organizers, and youth mentors.

Our Impact Journey

2012

The Beginning

Founded as Train A Child Today (TACT), providing school supplies to primary school students in rural communities.

2015

Expansion

Expanded to secondary school students and introduced mentorship programs, reaching more communities across Oyo State.

2019

New Programs

Launched free summer schools, scholarship programs, and the Community Resource Center in Ilora.

2025

Sustained Impact

Over 2,000 beneficiaries impacted, 7 communities reached, and 13 years of transforming lives through education and empowerment.
