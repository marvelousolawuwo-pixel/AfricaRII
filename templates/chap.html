Community Health Awareness Program

Improving Rural Healthcare Access

Problem Statement

Across rural communities in Africa, inadequate healthcare infrastructure and limited health education contribute to poor health outcomes. In Nigeria, only about 43% of rural residents have access to primary healthcare services, while maternal mortality remains alarmingly high at 512 deaths per 100,000 live births.

Our Approach

Health Education

Monthly awareness campaigns on maternal health, infectious disease prevention, nutrition, and sexual/reproductive health.

Strengthening PHCs

Partnering with local governments to equip Primary Health Centers with essential supplies and trained personnel.

Community Engagement

Establishing Health Committees and training local women as health ambassadors.

Research & Data

Collecting data on rural health trends to guide policy recommendations and interventions.

Support Community Health

Help us improve healthcare access in rural communities.
